Case study for ChatApp full stack project

Overview

ChatApp is a cutting-edge chat application designed to enhance communication and collaboration in the digital age. ChatApp aimed to provide users with a seamless messaging experience while prioritizing security and user privacy.

Purpose & Context

More and more people use their phones for daily tasks, such as shopping, creating to-do lists, communicating with friends, scheduling meetings, and more. That's why many companies offer native mobile versions of their web apps, or even skip creating a web app entirely.

Objectives

To build a chat app for mobile devices using React Native. The app will provide users with a chat interface and options to share images and their location.

Technical Requirements

I have written this app in React Native and i developed by using Expo. The chat conversations is stored in Google Firestore Database. The app let users to take pictures with their device's camera app and send them.

User Stories

The new user , who wants to be able to easily enter a chat room so can quickly start talking to his friends and family. The user will be able to to send messages to my friends and family members to exchange the latest news. Users will be sharing their locations with their friends and show them where they are .
